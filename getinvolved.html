Here are ways for you to make a PAWsitive Difference

Opportunity Details Action
Foster PAWsitive Difference needs loving foster homes! We do not have a central facility, so all of the dogs live in volunteer foster homes until they are ready for their forever home. If you can open your home to one of our adoptable dogs, please complete a foster application today!
Volunteer We need volunteers to help at adoption events, transport pets, intake fosters and many other things! We also have a program especially designed for teen volunteers. If you would like to help, please contact us!
Adopt To submit an adoption application, please visit the Adoptable Pets page and select the pet you are interested in.
Donate PAWsitive Difference accepts donations thru credit cards and cash apps.
Shop at Amazon PAWsitive Difference accepts donations in kind! Any supply that you can provide is a huge help. For a list of what we can use to provide better care for the pets, visit our Amazon Wishlist.
Shop at Smith's Through the Smith's Inspiring Donations, PAWsitive Difference recieves part of the proceeds everytime you shop at their store. For more information, visit the link.
